# EMRI: Astrophysics

## Formation Mechanisms

- Two-body relaxation (loss-cone scattering): high eccentricity orbits
- Hills mechanism: tidal binary disruption, one body captured
- AGN disk migration: Type I migration

Schwarzschild barrier suppresses resonant relaxation for low-eccentricity EMRIs.

## Event Rates

Typical: ~10^{-7}-10^{-6} yr^{-1} per galaxy

Relativistic correction (Feng & Tang 2026): relativistic loss-cone raises rate ~8x vs Newtonian.

LISA detection rate (Babak et al. 2017): few to few x10^3 per year (3 orders of magnitude astrophysical uncertainty).

## Environmental Effects

- AGN disk: migration torques modify orbital evolution; waveform imprint identifiable (Liu et al. 2026)
- Dark matter spike: dynamical friction + accretion modify inspiral (Zhang et al. 2024)
- Tidal resonances: nearby stars/BHs induce tidal resonances (Bonga et al. 2019)

## Cosmology

Dark sirens: EMRI provides absolute distance (GW) + statistical sky localization.
Single-event H0 precision ~10%; improves ~20% with disk modeling (Liu et al. 2026).
